Rules & Guidelines

  • Progressive overload is mandatory.
  • Maintain proper form — prioritize technique over weight.
  • Rest 60–90s between sets unless specified.
  • Track every session and mark exercises as done.
  • Stay hydrated and follow nutrition protocol.

Nutrition Guidelines

  • High protein: 1.8–2.2g per kg bodyweight.
  • Balanced carbs around workouts.
  • Healthy fats from nuts, seeds, avocado.
  • Hydration: 3–4L water daily.
  • Supplements: whey, BCAA, multivitamin as needed.
